My name is Jojo!

JoJo's Bizarre Devventure

I’m Johnver Laviña, but my friends know me as JoJo — and I hope we’ll get the chance to be friends, too.

I’m a Filipino artist who finds joy in bringing my imagination to life. I’ve always been passionate about learning new things, whether it's through crafting digital worlds, animation, or solving problems through code. For me, creativity isn’t limited to one form rather, it’s a language I speak through 3D modeling, visual effects, animation, game development, and programming.

My goal is simple: to create work that inspires, connects, and adds beauty to the world. Whether it’s building immersive environments, telling stories, or making games, I strive to grow a little more with every project.

Thank you for taking the time to explore my portfolio. I’m always excited to meet fellow creatives, collaborators, and curious minds — so don’t be shy, feel free to reach out and have a lovely day!
